# Day 2 — Thursday, March 5, 2026

## Building AI into Your Research and Teaching Practice

Day 2 shifts from understanding to building. Participants dive into agentic architectures, build their own AI research assistants, learn the legal and ethical guardrails, and progressively develop hands-on skills — culminating in a capstone workshop where everyone builds a working solution for their own research or teaching challenge.
